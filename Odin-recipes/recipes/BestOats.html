Best overnight oats

My go to breakfast, fast and simple.

Rich in protein and fiber, best of all, it takes little effort

We need:

  • 100g of Oats
  • Milk
  • 50g Chia seeds
  • Cut up fruits, berrys or jam of your choice

Workflow

  1. Find a bowl
  2. Insert the oats and chia seeds
  3. Add your favorite fruit or berrys (or both)
  4. Pour in the milk so it covers the surface and mix it all with a fork
  5. Refrigerate and enjoy in the morning
